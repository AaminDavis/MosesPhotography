About

Moses Photography was created based on the principles of natural beauty, and realist beauty standards are highly valued making us against hyper editing and photoshopping. We aim to help improve the public perception of body image, especially regarding women by presenting beauty and form in its unadulterated state.

Moses Photography is where photography is taken to the next level. Through the utilization of portraiture, stills and everyday items are taken to new heights. Moses Phototography incorporates industry techniques while adding his flare to showcase the beauty of each individual and item captured.

Moses Photography also showcases the beauty of the ordinary and mundane. We wish to enlighten our audience so that they will learn to take a step back and appreciate the beauty of their surroundings to stop society from taking it for granted.
